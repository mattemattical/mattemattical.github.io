Device Manual

Listed here is the Step – by – Step instructions on how to use the device.

1. Pour in the algae starter culture, nutrient solution, and fresh water in the feed container and mix them gently. Wait for a period of time until it is ready to be transported unto the Photobioreactor.

2. Once the mixture is in the Photobioreactor, the device will automatically monitor the conditions of the Algae inside to yield maximum growth. Statistical data will be displayed in the Web Application which also notifies the user if the algae is ready for the harvesting phase.

3. Solenoid Valves will then open and will proceed to the final harvesting stage. Here, the algal biomass will be filtered and separated from the water which can be used for the next cultivation process. The user can use an ample amount coming from the biomass as starter culture for the next cultivation process

4. The excess biomass will be sold to its rightful market in which it can be utilized in several applications (cosmetics, feedstocks, medicine, etc.)

5. Repeat Steps 1 to 4 in order to repeat the cycle of Algae Cultivation.
